    <h1>About Dogs</h1>
    <p>Dogs have been loyal companions to humans for centuries. There are various breeds of dogs, each with its own characteristics and temperament.</p>
    
    <h2>Types of Dogs</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Labrador Retriever</li>
        <li>Golden Retriever</li>
        <li>Siberian Husky</li>
        <li>Dachshund</li>
        <li>Shiba Inu</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Characteristics of Dogs</h2>
    <p>Dogs have an exceptional sense of smell, which is thousands of times more sensitive than that of humans. They also have excellent hearing, capable of detecting distant sounds and high-frequency noises.</p>
    
    <h2>Living with Dogs</h2>
    <p>Dogs are loyal and affectionate animals. With proper training and care, they make wonderful companions. Owning a dog requires daily walks, feeding, and health care.</p>
    
    <h2>Health Care for Dogs</h2>
    <p>Regular health check-ups and vaccinations are important. Providing a balanced diet and sufficient exercise is essential for maintaining their health. Pay attention to your dog's health and consult a veterinarian if you notice any unusual signs.</p>
